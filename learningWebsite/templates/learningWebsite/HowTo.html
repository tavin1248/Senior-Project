Welcome to the defect detection website!

Steps:

1) Seperate the non defective part images from the non defective part images.
2) Next, Split the defective part images into three categories: defective training images, defective testing images, and defective validation images.
3) Repeat step three with the non defective part images.
4) Create a new project and load all of the images into their respective categories.
5) Click the 'Train' button once all of the images have been uploaded and wait for the model to train.
6) Click the 'Evaluate model' button! The evaluation will show you how accurate and effective your model is at categorizing part defects.
7) Connect a Camera in order to make real time defect detections!
